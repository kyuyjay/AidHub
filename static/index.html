As the COVID-19 situation evolves, so does our community. This hub serves to help connect those who need resources with those who can offer them, by listing initiatives to redistribute items, services, money and opportunities across Singapore. Below, you can filter out the initiatives by category or type of aid you need or wish to contribute. If you want to add an initiative that you head or know about, please click the 'Submit an initiative' below!

If you are looking through this hub for ways to contribute resources, please remember that taking care of yourself is as important as taking care of your community. To prevent yourself from getting overwhelmed or burning out:

  1. Instead of browsing all the initiatives, form an idea of what you want to contribute, and then filter by category or type of aid.
  2. Before donating funds, evaluate your finances and create a budget for how much you can comfortably donate.

We are here for each other, for the long haul.

If you have any ideas for improving or distributing this hub, you can email Rachel Ooi. For technical related queries or improvements, you can email Yong Jian. Follow us on social media below.
